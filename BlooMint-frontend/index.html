Learn About Menstrual Hygiene 🩷

Menstrual Hygiene Basics

Wash your hands before and after handling menstrual products to help prevent infections. Clean your vulva gently with water; avoid scented soaps or internal douching that can disrupt your body’s natural balance. Change products frequently to stay fresh and healthy.

Choosing Your Product

There are many options—pads, tampons, menstrual cups, discs, period underwear. Choose based on your flow, comfort, convenience, and environmental preferences such as reusable cups or biodegradable pads.

Safe Disposal

Wrap used pads or tampons in paper or their wrappers and dispose of them in a bin. Never flush them, as this can clog plumbing and harm the environment.

Eco-Friendly Options

Reusable items like menstrual cups, washable cloth pads, or period underwear reduce waste. Biodegradable disposables are also available. Cloth pads made from organic materials can last years and can even be composted when worn out.

Understand Your Cycle

Tracking your cycle helps you know when your period starts, how long it lasts, and when symptoms like cramps or flow changes occur. This makes it easier to manage your hygiene and comfort. (You can also use BlooMint’s tracker!)
